Welcome to my website

Hello! My name is Clover. I've lived with Andrew for many, many years. I've moved across the east coast from Vermont to Pennsylvania. This is my Website that is full of information and photos about myself, so browse around and check out my pages on the about me, gallery of photos, and submit a comment in the comments section!

Diet changes

I've been experimenting with different foods lately. You see, my original food was my favorite thing ever, tuna. It was originally made in the USA and I loved it, but recently the company outsourced their manufacturing to Thailand, and ever since then, I hate the food! There's something different about it. I browsed online and saw many others with the same complaints.

Hope for the future?

After trying many different brands of new food, even the fancy ones, I still wasn't satisfied with the offerings of tuna. However, my grandmother found a can of wild-caught salmon from Target and, even though it was a store brand, I absolutely loved it. Now I can't get enough, and every morning, I bug Andrew for my half can of salmon! Thank god! I thought I was never going to find food for me again!
